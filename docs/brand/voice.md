# Volume Brand Voice

*Last updated: 2026-02-04*

Volume speaks with the confidence of a seasoned lifter—direct, no-nonsense, and focused on what matters.

## Brand Personality

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Direct** | Say what you mean. No fluff. |
| **Confident** | We know fitness. No hedging. |
| **Functional** | Everything serves a purpose. |
| **Brutalist** | Raw, honest, industrial aesthetic |
| **Respectful** | We trust users to know their goals |

## Tone Guidelines

### Do
- Use short, punchy sentences
- Speak in active voice
- Be specific, not vague
- Respect the user's time
- Use numbers and data
- Be encouraging without being cheesy

### Don't
- Use filler words (just, really, very)
- Hedge (might, maybe, possibly)
- Use jargon unnecessarily
- Be condescending or preachy
- Use excessive exclamation points
- Make false promises

## Example Copy

### Good ✅

> "Track your workout in under 60 seconds."

> "225 lbs × 8 reps. New PR."

> "No algorithms. No social features. Just your lifts."

> "You know your body. We just track the numbers."

### Bad ❌

> "We're so excited to help you on your amazing fitness journey!"

> "Volume might possibly help you maybe achieve your goals!"

> "Join thousands of users who love tracking their workouts with us!"

> "Are you ready to totally transform your life?!?!"

## Headlines & Taglines

### Primary Tagline
**"Track workouts in seconds, not minutes."**

### Alternative Headlines
- "BRUTALLY SIMPLE FITNESS"
- "NO FLUFF. JUST LIFTS."
- "LOG IT AND MOVE ON."
- "YOUR DATA. YOUR GOALS."

### Feature Headlines
- "ONE TAP LOGGING" (not "Super Easy Workout Tracking!")
- "WORKOUT HISTORY" (not "Your Amazing Progress Dashboard")
- "VOLUME TRACKING" (not "Intelligent Training Insights")

## Writing for Different Contexts

### App UI
- Ultra-concise
- Action-oriented
- UPPERCASE for labels/buttons
- Numbers in mono font

```
LOG SET
HISTORY
FINISH
```

### Error Messages
- Direct but helpful
- No blame language
- Offer next step

```
Connection failed. Check your internet and try again.
```

### Success States
- Brief acknowledgment
- Specific feedback

```
Set logged: 185 lbs × 10 reps
```

### Marketing Copy
- Slightly more personality allowed
- Still direct and confident
- Focus on benefits, not features

```
Volume gets out of your way. Log sets between rest periods. 
See your progress over time. No fluff.
```

### App Store Description
- Lead with value proposition
- Use bullet points
- Include social proof if authentic
- End with differentiator

```
Track your workouts in seconds, not minutes.

• One-tap set logging
• Real-time volume tracking
• Historical progress charts
• Offline-first design

Volume is for lifters who want to log and move on. 
No social features. No gamification. Just your data.
```

## Words We Use

| Concept | We Say | We Don't Say |
|---------|--------|--------------|
| Recording exercise | Log, Track | Journal, Chronicle |
| Exercise session | Workout | Training session, Routine |
| Amount lifted | Weight, Load | Resistance |
| Exercise | Exercise, Movement | Modality |
| User | You, Lifter | Athlete, Member |

## Voice Checklist

Before publishing any copy, ask:
1. Can I cut any words?
2. Is this direct and specific?
3. Would a busy lifter appreciate this?
4. Does this sound like a confident gym-goer?
5. Am I respecting the user's intelligence?
